📋 Placement Guidelines Portal
📌 Pre-Placement Process ⮟
✔️ Be punctual for all activities.
✔️ Attend pre-placement talks (PPTs) to be eligible.
✔️ Submit applications before deadlines.
✔️ You may apply to multiple companies, but once placed, you're out of the pool.
🎯 Selection Process ⮟
✔️ Accept the first offer immediately.
✔️ Backing out leads to blacklisting.
✔️ No offer negotiations unless allowed by company.
✔️ Absence in interviews = disqualification.
🌟 Dream & Super Dream Offers ⮟
✔️ Regular Offer: Basic job offer.
✔️ Dream: ≥ ₹7 LPA, Super Dream: ≥ ₹10 LPA.
✔️ You're allowed only 1 dream/super-dream offer.
✔️ Dual offers or upgrades depend on institute norms.
📑 Post-Placement Formalities ⮟
✔️ Complete formalities with company and TnP office.
✔️ Offer letters come through placement department.
✔️ Raise issues directly to the company HR only after TnP approval.
📌 General Instructions ⮟
✔️ Formal attire is mandatory for all placement activities.
✔️ Do not contact companies directly.
✔️ Job joining is post final exams, unless permitted.
✔️ Submit withdrawal form if exiting placement process.
✅ Best Practices for Students ⮟
✔️ Start early and work as a team.
✔️ Check your emails & notices regularly.
✔️ Update your resume with feedback.
✔️ Stay connected with your class reps & coordinators.
💰 Refundable Security Deposit ⮟
✔️ ₹5000 deposit is mandatory before placement starts.
✔️ Violation of rules leads to forfeit.
✔️ Refund after 6 months post graduation with proper documents.
✔️ Refund is via cheque or online transfer.
